I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Atmospheric and Oceanic Sciences at UCLA, working with [Andrew Stewart](https://dept.atmos.ucla.edu/stewart/home) and [Jim McWilliams](https://atmos.ucla.edu/jcm/). I am interested in a wide range of topics in geophysical fluid dynamics, including mesoscale eddies, topography-flow interaction, and overturning circulations. Before joining UCLA, I completed my Ph.D. at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology under the guidance of [Yan Wang](https://yanwanghkust.github.io/).
