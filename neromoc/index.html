NeroMOC Viewer

NeroMOC is a machine learning-enabled, satellite-based reconstruction of the Atlantic and Southern Ocean meridional overturning circulation in latitude-density space. This viewer summarizes the reconstructed streamfunction, reconstruction uncertainty, and estimated trend over April 2003 to April 2024.
